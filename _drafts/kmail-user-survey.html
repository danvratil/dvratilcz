KMail User Survey
Do you use KMail or Kontact? The KDE PIM developers want to get a better picture about how KMail is used to know which parts of the software they should focus on more and how they should evolve it in order to make it the best experience for everyone. You can help them by filling a short survey.
You can fill in the survey here.
The survey won't take more than 5 minutes of your time and it will be a big help for the developers. 
And please remember to share the survey with your family members, friends and colleagues who use KMail too!
